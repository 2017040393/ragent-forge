# Related Projects

RAGentForge is inspired by the broader RAG, agent, and evaluation ecosystem,
including:

- LangChain
- LangGraph
- LlamaIndex
- Haystack
- Dify
- RAGFlow
- AnythingLLM
- Ragas
- DeepEval

Those projects explore broad frameworks, orchestration, no-code workflows,
enterprise knowledge bases, and evaluation systems. RAGentForge has a narrower
early focus: stay small, local, inspectable, TUI-first, and learning-oriented.

The goal is not to replace those tools. The goal is to give developers a clear
local workbench for understanding how their own documents move through an
Agentic RAG workflow.
